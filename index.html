Treasure Hunt:

Description:

Participants will embark on a thrilling adventure to solve a series of clues scattered across both physical and digital locations. Each clue will test logical thinking, code-breaking, and puzzle-solving skills, requiring participants to work through complex challenges to uncover the next location or task.

Rules:

  • Teams are allowed to use search engines and computer programs to solve clues.
  • Any use of AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Copilot) will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Collaboration between teams is strictly prohibited.
  • Teams must work independently; any collaboration between teams is strictly prohibited.

The winning team will be the first to finish all the challenges and reach the final destination.

 CodeQuest: The Tech Treasure

Solve all the clues and progress through the series of challenges, with the ultimate goal of being the first team to complete the treasure hunt.

StyleSprint: The CSS Challenge


Description:

Unleash your creativity and dive into the world of web styling with StyleSprint! This challenge is designed to test your mastery over CSS. Participants will be tasked with transforming a basic, unstyled webpage into a stunning visual experience using only CSS. No additional JavaScript or libraries are allowed—just pure CSS magic!

Rules

  • No AI tools or internet searches are allowed.
  • Participants can only use basic web development tools.
  • The use of external libraries or frameworks is prohibited.
  StyleSprint: The CSS Challenge

Reproduce the given design accurately, ensuring responsiveness and adherence to best practices in CSS. Creativity and functionality are key to earning extra points.

Coding Challenge

Description:

This round will test participants' problem-solving skills with challenging questions based on Data Structures and Algorithms. Competitors will face a series of coding problems and will be required to write efficient code to solve them.

Rules

  • No AI assistance (e.g., ChatGPT) or internet searches are allowed.
  • Participants will use only the programming environment provided to them.
  • Plagiarism or copying code will lead to disqualification.
DSA Showdown

Solve all the DSA problems within the given time limit, focusing on both correctness and efficiency. Faster and more optimal solutions will receive higher scores.

The Tech Quiz

Description:

A multi-round quiz that covers a broad range of tech-related topics, including computer science, programming languages, and emerging technology trends. The competition will feature various rounds such as multiple-choice questions (MCQs), image-based questions, and rapid-fire rounds.

Rules

  • The quiz will have multiple rounds, including MCQs, picture, and rapid-fire rounds.
  • No internet searches or AI tools can be used.
  • Collaboration between teams is strictly prohibited.
  • Teams will compete head-to-head, and the team with the most points at the end of all rounds will be declared the winner.
BrainTeK: The Tech Quiz

Answer as many questions as possible to accumulate points, with a special emphasis on quick and accurate responses in the rapid-fire round.

Hackathon Challenge

Description:

A fast-paced hackathon event where teams will collaborate to develop a working prototype or solve a real-world problem using technology. The challenge may focus on areas like app development, web development, or addressing societal issues. Teams are encouraged to brainstorm creative solutions and build them within a limited time frame.

Rules

  • Teams must code their solution from scratch during the event; pre-built solutions are disqualified.
  • AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Copilot) are allowed only for brainstorming and ideation purposes, but not for writing code.
  • Internet searches are allowed for documentation and libraries, but any use of pre-existing codebases, templates, or libraries must be declared.
  • Each team will present their solution, and judges will evaluate the functionality, creativity, and impact of the project.
  Code Strom: The Hackathon

Create an innovative solution from scratch within the time limit. Teams will present a functional prototype at the end of the event, and extra points will be awarded for creativity and the practical application of the solution.

Electro Tresure Hunt

Description:

Participants will tackle intriguing electronic-based challenges and mind-bending puzzles, unraveling hidden clues to guide them on their quest. With each solved mystery, you’ll get closer to uncovering the ultimate treasure. Are you ready to outsmart the circuits and claim the prize

Rules

  • Team can consist of 2 to 3 members.
  • Individual participants are not allowed.
  • Each round will have a fixed time limit to complete the task or solve the puzzle.
  • Team must complete the current challenge to unlock the next clue.
  • Skipping any challenges is not allowed unless permitted by the organiser under special conditions.
  • Team must not interfere with other team's progress.
  • Any attempt to tamper with clue, tasks or circuits will lead to immediate disqualification.
Electro Tresure Hunt

Solve electronic challenges and puzzles to uncover clues and claim the ultimate treasure!

Circuit Debugging

Description:

Step into the role of an expert troubleshooter in this electrifying challenge! Participants will be presented with faulty electronic circuits and must identify the errors or incorrect components within a specific time limit. This task will test your knowledge, precision, and problem-solving abilities as you dive deep into the world of circuits. Can you debug the system and restore it to perfection before time runs out? Prove your mettle and showcase your technical prowess in this ultimate debugging showdown!

Rules

  • Participants are required to debug the circuit without external help.
  • Team may consist of 2 to 4 members.
  • Use only the tools provided to identify faults, such as misplaced components or broken connections.
  • Teams are not allowed to use personal devices like smartphones or laptops, unless specified by the organisers.
Circuit Debugging

Identify and fix faults in electronic circuits within a set time to showcase your debugging expertise
